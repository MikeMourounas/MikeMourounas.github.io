About

Hi, I'm Mike!

I am a Computational Linguist at Vantage Labs LLC, focusing on Grammar Error Correction and Natural Language Understanding.

After receiving my BA in English and Modern Greek from Rutgers in 2012, I moved to Tyrol, Austria where I worked as an Au Pair and immersed myself in the German language. I quickly realized two things that would come to shape my future: (i) I am a terrible skiier, and (ii) I had a knack for language and a desire to understand the language faculty in greater depth.

I then moved to London to pursue post-graduate studies in Linguistics at University College London. I received my MA in 2015, and my PhD in 2019. My research focused on the formal semantics of co-reference and the interfaces between semantics and morpho-syntax.

In August of 2019 I accepted my position at Vantage, and currently live in Bucks County, Pennsylvania. When I'm not languaging, I can often be found dabbling in website design with Django, practicing for my next 5k, or prepping for the odd D&D one-shot.
